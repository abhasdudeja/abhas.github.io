About TMM

Problem Statement:
Data in Indian Context is really difficult to procure due to the laborious nature of the exercise, involving various organizations, departments and officials.

Solution:
The Map Man is an initiative to provide Geo-spatial data for India. The data is compiled and collated through various years.

The Initiative hopes to provide free access to the data to researchers, academicians and students alike through basic authentication.
